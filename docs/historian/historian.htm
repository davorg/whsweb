Historian Issue 103

The archaeology and history of Wandsworth

The Wandsworth Historian

The Wandsworth Historical Society publishes the Wandsworth Historian, its much-respected local history journal, twice a year. 

The most recent issue is No. 103, Spring 2017. To read a press release from the editor click here.

This issue contains articles on the following subjects:

  • The Australian war graves in Wandsworth Cemetery
  • Wandsworth and the Spanish Civil War
  • Putney Old Burial Ground

The magazine is free to members and costs £3.00 + £1.50 postage for non-members.   

Here are three articles chosen from our journal's recent past to show the range and quality of research that the Society publishes:

  • 'The Abbey, the Monk and Domesday Wandsworth' by Keith Bailey which first appeared in the Wandsworth Historian in 2011 [LINK]
  • 'The Watermen's Chest of Putney' by Dorian Gerhold which first appeared in the Wandsworth Historian in 2013 [LINK]
  • 'A Battersea School Prize from the 1870s' by Nicola Hale, Neil Robson and David Ainsworth which first appeared in the Wandsworth Historian in 2015 [LINK].

We hope you find them interesting.
